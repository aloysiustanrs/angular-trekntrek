TrekNTrack CCA

Our objective is to develop the next generation's passion towards wildlife, equipping them holistically through a new CCA.

As we Trek through our Green City, we will Track all our efforts in progressing as a Green Nation

Benefits

Safety Educating students on the importance of safety when interacting with wildlife and natural environments Ethics Foster a sense of respect and responsibility for wildlife among students Identification Familiarizing students with the ecosystems present in their local environment First Aid Teaching students essential first aid skills specifically tailored for wildlife Soft + Hard Skills Foster students' creativity, event management abilities, and information technology skills through activities in the CCA Application through action Equipping students with knowledge, techniques and the opportunity to protect our ecosystems
